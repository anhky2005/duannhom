In celebration of World Environment Day, June 5th, 2023!

In 2023, the United Nations Environment Programme (UNEP) announced the theme for World Environment Day as 'Solutions to Plastic Pollution', with a focus on implementing the campaign 'Beat Plastic Pollution'. According to UNEP's assessment, environmental pollution from plastic waste has become one of the most pressing issues that countries must address. Every year, the world produces approximately 400 million tons of plastic, half of which is single-use plastic, and less than 10% is recycled. It is estimated that around 19 to 23 million tons of plastic waste are released into lakes, rivers, and oceans each year

Changing public awareness and raising people's consciousness about waste sorting at source is a challenging task that requires a long-term process, along with effective mechanisms for operation and violation handling.
