3. Consulting on Waste Treatment and Recycling

Nghe An Environmental Treatment Joint Stock Company offers expert consultation on selecting the most suitable waste treatment methods for various types of waste. For solid waste, we recommend solutions such as sanitary landfills, incineration, recycling, and composting, depending on the specific needs and environmental impact. For liquid waste, we employ biological treatment, chemical-physical treatment, and recycling for reuse to ensure effective and sustainable management. Hazardous waste is carefully classified, packaged safely, and treated by specialized units to prevent harm to the environment and public health. Household waste management focuses on source separation, efficient collection, and integration for recycling, making it easier to minimize waste and maximize resource recovery. Additionally, we develop comprehensive recycling plans to help save resources and reduce costs. Our approach involves assessing the recyclability of different waste types, organizing separate sorting and collection of recyclable materials, and choosing the most appropriate recycling technologies. Through these processes, we convert waste into valuable resources, including plastics, paper, organics, and more, contributing to environmental sustainability and circular economy goals.

. 4 Training and Raising Environmental Awareness

The objective of the training program is to enhance the awareness and responsibility of employees regarding environmental protection, resource efficiency, and to promote sustainable production and consumption behaviors within the company. The training courses are specifically designed based on the actual needs of the employees and include essential content such as basic knowledge of the environment and sustainable development, environmental protection laws and related regulations, and measures to save energy, water, and other resources. Additionally, employees will be trained on waste classification and reduction at the source, chemical safety, hazardous waste treatment, cleaner production practices, and fostering an environmental culture within businesses. The training will be conducted through various methods, including in-person sessions at the company or focused workshops, as well as online training via accessible platforms. The program combines both theoretical lessons and practical activities, including simulation scenarios, to ensure an interactive and effective learning experience. The training will also be regularly updated to keep employees informed about the latest knowledge and global green trends. The benefits of this training program are significant, as it helps employees understand their role in environmental protection, enhances resource management efficiency, and improves environmental risk control. It also contributes to building the company's image as environmentally responsible, while providing a solid foundation for the successful implementation of an environmental management system (ISO 14001)
