üè≠ Hoang Mai Cement Plant

Hoang Mai Cement Plant is one of the major cement manufacturing facilities in the North Central region of Vietnam. Equipped with modern technology and boasting a large-scale production capacity, the plant plays a pivotal role in supplying cement for both civil and industrial construction projects throughout the country. Central to its operations is the clinker production line, which produces the main component used in cement manufacturing. In this process, the plant utilizes F0 fuel oil as the primary energy source for its rotary kiln system. F0 oil, being a liquid fossil fuel with a high calorific value, ensures a stable and efficient heating process during clinker firing ‚Äî a critical stage in cement production. To maintain seamless operations and avoid interruptions, Hoang Mai Cement Plant implements a scheduled fuel supply plan. This plan ensures that F0 fuel oil is delivered regularly according to a predefined timeline, which helps prevent production delays, enhances operational efficiency, and reduces risks related to fuel shortages. The effective use and strategic management of fuel not only support the plant‚Äôs stable productivity but also contribute to optimizing production costs. This, in turn, strengthens the plant‚Äôs competitiveness in the increasingly demanding cement market.

The fleet operating at Cua Lo Port mainly consists of domestic and coastal cargo vessels responsible for transporting goods such as construction materials, steel, cement, fertilizers, and consumer products.
